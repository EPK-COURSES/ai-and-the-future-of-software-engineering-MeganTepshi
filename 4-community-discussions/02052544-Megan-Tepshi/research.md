# Research
The first public release of AI helping tools, was introduced to the mankind in 2022 with the release of ChatGPT and GitHub Copilot. Since then the hypothetical question related to software engineers “disappearing” has moved from speculations to a threating concern.This research article strictly reviews the impact of AI with precise evidence, based on observational analyses of developers communities, that are gathered between 2023 and early 2026.
The debate is split into two primary schools of thoughts. Both sides are aware of the frequent AI adoption, but whether humans will remain the center of Software Engineering is what they disagree on. The most significant data findings from public developers discussions show that generative AI is mainly used for the basic technical requirements such as coding. Consequently, human engineers are responsible for requirements engineering, complex architecture, ethical evaluation and human collaboration. Many software engineers view the implementation of AI as a "propoganda" of CEOs as a marketing method.
